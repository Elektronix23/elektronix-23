ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION DEPARTMENT

This is one of the most sought-after engineering courses in India after Computer science, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. Electronics and Communication Engineering involves designing, developing, and manufacturing electronic equipment (for devices and circuits) as well as communication systems. The course shares diverse areas from creative, interaction, embodiment designing to instrumentation, robotics, and automation. It covers basic engineering concepts and techniques like engineering mathematics, drawing, physics ,chemistry. Electronics and Communication Engineering change lives, it makes life more efficient and this very thought can induce us to pursue this course. This undergraduate course encourages critical thinking and market research which are life-enhancing skills.
